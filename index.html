I am a Ph.D. student at the Ultra-Intelligent Computing/Communication Laboratory (UCLab), Chung-Ang University, under the supervision of Prof. Sungrae Cho. My research focuses on Machine Learning, Semantic Communication, and Ubiquitous Computing. Previously, I completed my M.S. at Chung-Ang University with a thesis on bidirectional slimmable semantic communication, and my B.S. at Troy University as Class of 2023 Valedictorian.
